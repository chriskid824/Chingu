**Chingu - Meet New Friends Over Dinner**

**Subtitle:** Social Eating & Dating App

**Description:**

Hungry for connection? Chingu is a unique social app that brings people together through the love of food. Whether you're looking for a dinner date, a new friend, or a foodie companion, Chingu makes it easy to meet people in a safe and relaxed environment.

**Key Features:**

*   **Dinner Matching:** Swipe to find people who share your taste in food and interests.
*   **Curated Events:** Discover and join dinner events at top-rated restaurants in your city.
*   **Safe Community:** Verified profiles and a supportive community ensure a safe experience.
*   **Real Connections:** Skip the small talk and bond over a shared meal.
*   **Chat & Plan:** Easily coordinate dinner plans with our built-in chat feature.

**Why Chingu?**

In a digital world, real connections happen offline. Chingu bridges the gap by using food as the universal language. Join thousands of users who are making meaningful connections, one meal at a time.
